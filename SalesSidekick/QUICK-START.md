# SalesSidekick — Quick Start

Get up and running in under 5 minutes. No technical setup required.

---

## Step 1: Install the Plugin

1. Download `SalesSidekick.zip` from [pipelinerebel.com/download](https://pipelinerebel.com/download)
2. Open Claude Desktop
3. Go to **Settings** > **Plugins** > **Add Plugin** > **Upload a File**
4. Select the `SalesSidekick.zip` file you downloaded

That's it. The plugin is installed.

---

## Step 2: Create Your Workspace

SalesSidekick saves everything to a folder on your machine — your deals, contacts, call notes, and all your data. Create a folder and set it up as a Cowork Project:

1. Create a folder called `SalesSidekick` in your Documents folder
2. In Claude Desktop, click the **+** icon next to **Projects** in the left sidebar
3. Name the project `SalesSidekick` and point it at the folder you just created

**Why Documents?** If you use iCloud, Google Drive, or OneDrive, your Documents folder is probably backed up automatically. That means your SalesSidekick data is protected without any extra setup.

---

## Step 3: Start Your First Conversation

Open a conversation inside your SalesSidekick project and say hello.

SalesSidekick will:
1. Introduce itself and explain what it can do
2. Check what connectors you have (Gmail, Calendar, etc. — all optional)
3. Ask the basics: your name, company, what you sell
4. Offer seven quick style questions so it can match how you work (optional — takes 2 minutes)
5. Research your company and competitive landscape automatically
6. Save your identity so every future session knows you from the first message

**Time to first value: about 5 minutes.**

---

## Your First Five Minutes

SalesSidekick is voice-first. You don't need commands or special syntax — just talk like you'd talk to a colleague.

**Two great ways to start getting value immediately:**

**1. Share your top active deals**
> "Here are my top 3 deals right now: Acme is in tech eval, Globex is renewing, and Initech just started discovery"

Or paste a screenshot of your CRM or forecast. SalesSidekick will build context around each deal and tell you what needs attention.

**2. Drop in a recent call transcript**
> "Here's a transcript from my call with Acme today"

Paste the text. SalesSidekick will score the deal, pull out every action item, write your follow-up email, and flag risks — all at once.

---

## What You Can Ask At Any Time

Not sure what to say? Try these:
- **"What's on my plate today?"** — morning briefing with deals needing attention and tasks due
- **"Prep me for my call with Acme"** — intel brief with talking points and gaps to probe
- **"I just finished a call with..."** — paste a transcript and get the full debrief
- **"Write a follow-up email to Sarah at Acme"** — contextual email in your voice
- **"What's my forecast looking like?"** — pipeline breakdown with risk flags
- **"Research this company for me"** — deep account research with evidence grading
- **"Build a business case for Acme"** — executive-level POV with financial angles
- **"What can you do?"** — full explanation of everything SalesSidekick knows how to do

---

## What Happens Behind the Scenes

Every morning (if you set it up during your first conversation), SalesSidekick checks your deals and generates a briefing before you even open your laptop. It looks for:

- Deals that haven't moved in a while
- Missing decision-makers on important deals
- Forecasts that don't match the evidence
- Tasks that are overdue
- Anything that needs your attention

You'll see this briefing when you open your SalesSidekick project and ask "what's on my plate?"

## Tips

- **The first few sessions take a little longer** as you build up context. That's normal — it gets faster every time.
- **You can always change how it works.** If an email sounds off, tell it. If you want a different format, ask. SalesSidekick adapts to your feedback.
- **Drop anything in, any format.** A CRM screenshot, a copied email, a raw transcript, a company one-pager — just paste it. SalesSidekick figures out what it is.
- **Optional connectors:** Gmail (send emails directly), Google Calendar (meeting-aware briefings), Google Drive (find transcripts automatically). None required. See [CONNECTORS.md](CONNECTORS.md) for setup.
- **Upgrades are easy.** Download the new zip, upload it to Plugins. Your workspace data and personal settings are untouched — only the plugin brain gets updated.
