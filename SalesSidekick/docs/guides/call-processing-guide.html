Call Processing

What happens when you paste a call transcript into SalesSidekick

1

You paste a transcript

Any format works — raw text, a copied doc, meeting notes, even a voice memo summary. Just paste it and say "I just finished a call with Acme."

2

SalesSidekick reads the full conversation

Identifies who was on the call, what was discussed, what commitments were made, what objections came up, and what competitive intel surfaced.

3

Runs through 6 analysis frameworks simultaneously

MEDDPICC scoring, task extraction, coaching analysis, email drafting, risk detection, and competitive intelligence — all in one pass.

4

Produces 6 outputs and saves everything

Each output is actionable. Your deal record, contact records, and task list are all updated automatically.

auto-saved Call note with extracted intelligence
auto-saved Deal record updated with new MEDDPICC scores
auto-saved Contact records updated with last activity
created Action items as tracked tasks

The 6 Outputs

1. MEDDPICC Scoring

Each of the 8 qualification elements scored Red, Yellow, or Green — with evidence from the call for every change.

2. Action Items

Every commitment, follow-up, and next step extracted and saved as tracked tasks with due dates.

3. Coaching Feedback

5 dimensions scored 1-5: discovery quality, objection handling, rapport building, next-step clarity, and talk ratio.

4. Follow-Up Email

Ready-to-send follow-up drafted in your voice. Summarizes key points, confirms next steps, keeps momentum.

5. Risk Signals

Flags anything concerning: stalled champion, budget uncertainty, competitor mention, timeline slip, missing stakeholder.

6. Competitive Intel

Any competitor mentioned is captured. If a battlecard exists, displacement angles are surfaced immediately.

How to use it

Just paste your transcript and tell SalesSidekick who the call was with. Any format works — you don't need to clean it up or format it. Say something like "I just got off a call with Sarah at Acme — here's the transcript" and paste it in. Everything else is automatic.
